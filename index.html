INTRODUCTION

Many call us teachers or educators, but what we really do is facilitate learning, spark curiosity, lead to adventure, and create possibilities students may have never considered.  In the field of information, communication, and technology, this world is constantly changing, expanding, and mind blowing.  The key to success is a willingness to constantly learn.

 

Code.org is what we use for the Computer Science Discoveries course.

THOUGHTS TO PONDER

  • Computer science is no more about computers than astronomy is about telescopes.
  • The question of whether a computer can think is like the question of whether submarines can swim.
  • Treat your password like a toothbrush--don't let anyone else use it and change it every six months.
  • Relax, it's only ones and zeros.
  • CAPS LOCK: preventing logins since 1980.
  • If at first you don't succeed, call it version 1.0
